Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for groups of 4 or more. Please call us at least 24 hours in advance to secure your table.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver within a 5-mile radius. Orders can be placed through our website or by calling directly.

Are you catering?

Absolutely! We provide catering services for events. Contact our catering team for custom packages and pricing.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options across all our menu categories.

Can you accommodate allergies?

We take allergies seriously. Please inform our staff of any dietary restrictions, and we'll prepare your meal accordingly.

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, digital wallets, and online payment methods for your convenience.
